#+title: Exercises

* Info

This repo contains a collection of exercises, problems, mini-projects and general tinkerings.

Solutions could be as recent as yesterday or as old as half a decade. As such, they may be outdated, and likely wrong and unlikely to be updated. They are each meant to serve as a learning exercise for myself and an indicator of progress, not to boast any programming prowess or as a solution bank.
