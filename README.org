#+title: Exercises

* Info

This repo contains a collection of exercises, problems, mini-projects and general tinkerings.

Solutions could be as recent as yesterday or as old as years. As such, they may be outdated, and likely wrong and unlikely to be updated. They are each meant to serve as a learning exercise, not to boast any programming prowess or as a solution bank.
